About the Blog

In the modern world, our access to information is unprecedented. You’re practically a Google search and some time-investment away from understanding anything you have a capacity for. I’m interested in seeing if I can actually change my career with a self-led curriculum of online courses.

I created the blog as a project during an online Python course, and thought I should put it to use. After all, a major role of the traditional university is to open your ideas up for collaboration and criticism.

“If I have seen further it is by standing on the shoulders of Giants” - Isaac Newton 1675

The blog is meant simply to be a form of expression, If you find it helpful or hurtful let me know. Let’s not go through it, let’s grow through it.
