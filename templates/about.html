About the Blog

In the modern world, our access to information is unprecedented. You’re practically a Google search and some time-investment away from understanding anything you have a capacity for. I’m interested in seeing if I can actually change my profession with a self-led curriculum of online courses.

I created the blog during a python course and thought it might be a good way to not only share some thoughts, but also sharpen my understanding. After all, to open your ideas up for collaboration and criticism is a necessary process for understanding, and is a major detriment of not attending a university.

“If I have seen further it is by standing on the shoulders of Giants” - Isaac Newton 1675

I’m excited to share my journey here!
